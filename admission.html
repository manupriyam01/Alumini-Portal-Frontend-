Admission Procedure

The institute offers 3 years diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering branch.

Eligibility

SSLC/10th standard pass
OR
P.U.C Pass/Fail
OR
I.T.I pass in relevant Grade
OR
Any other equivalent courses Recognized by Govt. of Karnataka. All the eligible candidates may apply to the college in the application form available in the college office. Admissions is purely based on first come first serve basis.
