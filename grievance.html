ABOUT GRIEVANCE READDRESSAL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

We maintain and manages all the complaints. This GRIEVANCE READDRESSAL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM is an interactive solution for solving the complaints online which saves lots of time for students.it is an efficient platform designed to address and resolve complaints from students, staff, and other stakeholders. It ensures transparency, accountability, and timely resolution through features like online grievance submission, status tracking, notifications, and an escalation mechanism. this system promotes a supportive and responsive environment in the college.

PROCEDURE IN READDRESSAL OF GRIEVANCES

  1. On receipt of an online complaint, the institution shall refer the complaint to the appropriate Student Grievance Redressal Committee, along with its comments within 15 days of receipt of complaint on the online portal.
  2. The Student Grievance Redressal Committee, as the case may be, shall fix a date for hearing the complaint which shall be communicated to the institution and the aggrieved student. An aggrieved person may appear either in person or authorize a representative to present the case.
  3. Grievances not resolved by the Student Grievance Redressal Committee shall be referred to the Ombudsperson, within the time period provided in these regulations.
  4. Institutions shall extend co-operation to the Ombudsperson or the Student Grievance Redressal Committee, in early redressal of grievances; and failure to do so may be reported by the Ombudsperson to the Council, which shall take action in accordance with the provisions of these regulations.
  5. The Ombudsperson shall, after giving reasonable opportunities of being heard to both parties, on the conclusion of proceedings, pass such order, with reasons there for as may be deemed fit to redress the grievance and provide such relief as may be appropriate to the aggrieved student. (viii) The institution, as well as the aggrieved student, shall be provided with copies of the order under the signature of the Ombudsperson, and the institution shall place it for general information on its website.
  6. The institution shall comply with the recommendations of the Ombudsperson; and the Ombudsperson shall report to the Council any failure on the part of the institution to comply with the recommendations.
  7. The Ombudsperson may recommend appropriate action against the complainant, where a complaint is found to be false or frivolous.

Here are some General Rules of Grievance Readdressal Management System

Ensure the system is easily accessible to all students, providing multiple channels for filing grievances, such as in-person, online portals, email, or helplines.

Ensure transparency in the grievance redressal process to build trust and fairness among stakeholders.

Maintain confidentiality to protect the identity and details of the complainant.

Ensure fair treatment to all parties involved in the grievance resolution process.

Address grievances promptly to minimize inconvenience and ensure resolution.

Ensure that grievances are handled with adequate responsiveness and attention.

Hold all involved parties accountable for their roles in grievance redressal.

Maintain proper documentation of grievances and their resolution process.

Provide a robust appeal mechanism for students dissatisfied with the resolution.

Conduct periodic reviews of grievance redressal mechanisms for efficiency.

Train personnel involved in the grievance redressal process for effective handling.

Ensure that there is no retaliation against those who file grievances.
